    Project information

    • Category: Branding
    • Client: Casa Estrella Norte
    • Project date: October 2024
    • Project URL: https://st3adyp1ck.github.io/EstrellaNorte

    Project Focus: Logo and Custom Graphics Design

    The branding for Casa Estrella Norte draws from its vibrant cultural roots in San Cristóbal. Incorporating Mayan motifs and compass imagery, the designs reflect both local tradition and global adventure. Each element, from the striking compass logo to the intricate patterns on the building, symbolizes guidance, exploration, and a welcoming spirit. The bright colors and detailed aesthetics aim to evoke a sense of warmth and discovery, aligning with the hostel’s mission to offer a unique, immersive experience for travelers.
